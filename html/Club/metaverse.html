    WHAT IS THE METAVERSE?


    Metaverse is a concept of an endless, online, 3D universe that combines multiple virtual spaces. You can see the metaverse as a future iteration of the Internet. With metaverse, users have the ability to work, meet, Play games and socialize with each other in these 3D spaces.
    Facebook is one of the strongest advocates of creating a unified metaverse. This is very interesting for the crypto-based metaverse thanks to Facebook's Diem stablecoin project.
